The Landmark 81 in Vietnam building has 81 floors and a gross floor area of 241,000 square meters. According to the designer, the tower's design is inspired by the traditional bamboo bundle which represents strength and unity in Vietnamese culture.


Amusement and recreational activities:
  • Landmark 81 Skyview:

    • The whole observatory is surrounded by a transparent glass system, which allows visitors to see the entire city of Ho Chi Minh from above. Furthermore, the sky view observatory has a dining area, cafe, and thrilling games.


  • Infinity pool:

    • The infinity pool is 120 square meters wide and has stunning views of the Saigon River. It is specially designed to resemble a lake that extends to the horizon, bringing a great feeling of relaxation to tourists.


  • Cafes, restaurants with good view:

    • Landmark 81 in Vietnam is a culinary paradise with 30 restaurants from all over the world. is a gathering place for many well-known, long-standing coffee brands that serve delicious and high-quality drinks.
