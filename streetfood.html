Ho Chi Minh City (HCMC) is renowned for its vibrant street food culture, offering a diverse array of delicious and affordable dishes. Exploring the city's street food markets is a fantastic way to immerse yourself in the local culinary scene. Here are a few street food markets in HCMC that you might want to explore:


  • Ben Thanh Market:

    • Ben Thanh Market is one of the city's most iconic landmarks and a bustling marketplace. While known for its variety of goods, the surrounding streets come alive with numerous street food stalls during the day and evening. You can find local specialties like pho, banh mi, com tam, and fresh tropical fruits


  • Ho Thi Ky Market

    • Ho Thi Ky Market used to be a flower market in its early days. Nowadays, flowers are still the most prominent item sold there. When you visit Ho Thi Ky Market, you will encounter hundreds of colorful and fragrant flowers. Ho Thi Ky Market is not only a flower market, but also a famous snack paradise for Saigon's youth. The food market at Ho Thi Ky Market opens from 3pm until late at night. There are hundreds of food stalls offering a variety of delicious and attractive dishes for you to choose from.
